# about me

hello, i'm philipp — a senior full-stack engineer in berlin. i help small engineering teams ship faster by making the right architecture calls, clearing blockers, and integrating ai where it actually saves time.

i co-founded [fit analytics](https://www.fitanalytics.com), which i scaled from prototype to market leader in online size recommendations (used by asos, uniqlo, the north face). after that, i led development at [codepan](https://codepan.de) on projects for daimler, bosch, wix, and webasto — managing architecture decisions and team direction across very different domains.

most recently, i've been freelancing on ai-powered products: an llm-driven recruiting tool with automated candidate screening, a financial document analysis system that replaces manual compliance checks, and airspace management software in ocaml.

## how i work

- **take products from mvp to scalable platform** — i've done it multiple times, from early-stage prototypes to data-driven web apps serving millions
- **integrate ai into real workflows** — document processing, candidate screening, compliance checks — not demos, but systems that replace hours of manual work
- **lead small teams** — i prioritize, translate stakeholder needs into tech requirements, and create focus so the team ships

i anticipate challenges, clear obstacles before they slow progress, and create environments where teams do their best work.

## tools & interests

my stack: **typescript**, **next.js**, **react**, **python**, **django**, **fastapi**, and **postgres**. i also work with ocaml, rust, and swift when the project calls for it. deeply interested in computer linguistics, nlp, and making llm integrations genuinely useful.

multilingual: german (native), english & romanian (fluent), hebrew & french (fair), indonesian (basic).

## side projects

i'm a compulsive builder. current projects include [thefoodie.space](https://www.thefoodie.space) (next.js + ai data pipeline for food creators), [commentable.space](https://www.commentable.space) (self-hosted commenting with ai moderation), [laundryroom.social](https://www.laundryroom.social) (a free open-source meetup alternative), [pleeboo](https://www.pleeboo.com) (who-brings-what for events), and [4fram.es](https://4fram.es) (a lomo camera gif tool).
