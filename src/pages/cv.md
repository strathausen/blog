## Profile

I help teams ship AI-powered products — from idea to production. With 16+ years of full-stack experience, I specialize in building LLM integrations that genuinely work, not just impressive demos.

I co-founded Fit Analytics (now a market leader in online size recommendations used by ASOS, Uniqlo, The North Face) and led development at Codepan on projects for Daimler, Bosch, Wix, and Webasto. Currently freelancing on AI-powered products across recruiting, energy compliance, and aerospace.

I excel in fast-paced environments by identifying pragmatic solutions that balance immediate results with long-term maintainability.

My tools of choice: **TypeScript**, **React**, **Next.js**, **Node.js**, **Postgres**, and **Python** — plus **OCaml** and **Rust** when the project calls for it.
